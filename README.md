HireIQ is an AI-powered interview preparation platform that helps students and job seekers practice technical and HR interviews through realistic mock interview simulations.

The platform analyzes a candidate's resume, selected job role, and experience level to generate personalized interview questions, evaluate responses, and provide detailed performance feedback.

## ✨Features

- 📄 Resume Upload & Parsing
- 🤖 AI-Generated Interview Questions
- 🎯 Role-Based & Skill-Based Question Generation
- 🎤 Voice Interaction (Text-to-Speech & Speech-to-Text)
- 📊 AI-Powered Performance Evaluation
- 📈 Interview Analytics Dashboard
- 📑 Downloadable PDF Reports
- 🔐 Secure Authentication with Firebase
- 💳 Credit & Subscription Management using Razorpay
- 📚 Interview History Tracking
- 🌙 Light & Dark Mode Support
- 📱 Fully Responsive Design

## 📌 How It Works

1. User registers or logs in.
2. Uploads resume (PDF).
3. Resume is parsed to extract skills and experience.
4. User selects target role and experience level.
5. AI generates personalized interview questions.
6. User answers via text or voice.
7. AI evaluates responses based on:
   - Communication
   - Confidence
   - Correctness
8. Performance report and analytics are generated.
9. User can download detailed PDF reports and track progress over time.
